English (英語):
It's a beautiful day today.

語彙分解 (Word Breakdown)

It's - it is beautiful - 美しい (utsukushii) day - 日 (hi) today - 今日 (kyou)

文法説明 (Grammar Explanation)

"It's a …" is a common structure for describing something. The verb "is" is contracted to "'s".
